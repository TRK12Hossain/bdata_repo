## 1. CORE EDITORIAL PHILOSOPHY

### The Mission
Present data-driven stories about Bangladesh's political, economic, and social trajectory in a way that is:
- **Strictly factual** â€” every claim is sourced and verifiable
- **Genuinely neutral** â€” no party, ideology, or era is praised or blamed
- **Accessible** â€” complex statistics translated into human stories
- **Trustworthy** â€” when data is uncertain, we say so

### The One Rule That Overrides Everything
> If a piece of content cannot be published without a credible, verifiable source, it does not get published.

Trust is the product. A single uncited or wrong statistic destroys credibility that takes months to build.

## 3. WHAT WE DO NOT COVER (Hard Rules)

### Absolute Prohibitions â€” Never, Under Any Circumstances

| Prohibited Topic | Why |
|---|---|
| Active court cases / ongoing legal proceedings | Cannot comment on sub-judice matters |
| Personal lives of politicians or public figures | We cover data, not people |
| Religious controversies or sectarian tensions | Outside our mission; high risk of harm |
| Unverified allegations or rumors | No source = no publication |
| Speculation about future elections | Inherently partisan |
| Content that identifies or targets specific individuals | Privacy and safety concern |
| Any content that could incite communal tension | Zero tolerance |

### Soft Prohibitions â€” Avoid Unless Exceptional Circumstances

| Topic | Why Avoid | Exception |
|---|---|---|
| Active political controversies (ongoing protests, political arrests) | Too high-risk for misinterpretation | Only if 3rd-party data (not media reports) available |
| Topics with no verifiable government or international data | We can't fact-check opinion | Only if a recognized research institution (BRAC, CPD) has published findings |
| Very recent events (< 2 weeks old) | Data not yet settled or verified | Only if official government data already published |
| Content comparing leadership or personalities | Veers into personality politics | Stick to policy outcomes with data |

## 5. DATA QUALITY STANDARDS

### Minimum Bar for Publication
A data point can only be used in content if it meets ALL of the following:

- [ ] Source is Tier 1 or Tier 2 (see `source_priority.md`)
- [ ] Source URL or document title is recorded
- [ ] Data was published by the source within the last 5 years (or is clearly historical)
- [ ] If the source is a government portal, cross-referenced with at least one independent source
- [ ] Methodology is understood (e.g., what does "poverty headcount" mean in BBS context?)

## 7. TOPIC IDEA GENERATION RULES (For Content Strategist Agent)

When the Strategist Agent generates weekly topic suggestions, it must follow these filters:

### Include Ideas That...
- Have verifiable data from at least one Tier 1 or Tier 2 source
- Cover a topic relevant to Bangladesh's trajectory (economic, social, infrastructure)
- Can be framed without triggering any sensitivity flag
- Are timely (connected to recent data release, anniversary, or current event â€” but not breaking news)
- Appeal to a young (18-35) Bangladeshi audience trying to understand their country

### Reject Ideas That...
- Have no verifiable data available
- Require naming political parties as subjects of success/failure
- Cover any absolute prohibition topic (Section 3)
- Are purely opinion-based (no statistics possible)
- Are too niche or technical for a general social media audience

### Weekly Idea Mix Target
| Category | Target Share |
|---|---|
| Economics / Finance | 25-30% |
| Education | 15-20% |
| Healthcare / Social | 15-20% |
| Infrastructure | 10-15% |
| International Comparisons | 10-15% |
| Historical / Timeline | 10-15% |

## 8. HUMAN ESCALATION TRIGGERS

The pipeline must automatically pause and notify the human operator when:

1. Any sensitivity flag (Section 6) is triggered
2. Fact-check confidence score falls below 90%
3. No Tier 1 or Tier 2 source found for a key claim
4. Content writer fails style check after 2 retries
5. Research agent cannot find any data after trying all fallback sources
6. A topic has been in "data unavailable" status for more than 7 days
